## 🎓 KNOWLEDGE BASE

### Understanding Terms

**SEO (Search Engine Optimization)**
- Getting website to rank #1 in Google

**CTR (Click-Through Rate)**
- % of people who click your link in search results
- Goal: 2-3%+

**DA (Domain Authority)**
- Score 1-100 indicating website's authority
- Goal: Backlinks from DA40+

**LSI Keywords**
- Semantically related keywords
- Help Google understand topic

**Featured Snippet**
- Position 0 (above organic results)
- Worth 10x normal traffic

**Core Web Vitals**
- LCP (Largest Contentful Paint): <2.5s
- FID (First Input Delay): <100ms
- CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift): <0.1

## ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: How long until I rank #1?**
A: 3-6 months for top 10, 6-12 months for top 3 (depending on competition)

**Q: Do I need to pay for SEO?**
A: No, organic SEO is free. But backlinks & content creation take time/money.

**Q: When will I see traffic?**
A: Usually 4-6 weeks for first impressions, 8-12 weeks for meaningful traffic

**Q: Should I buy backlinks?**
A: No! Google penalizes paid link schemes. Build naturally via guest posts, etc.

**Q: How often should I publish content?**
A: Consistency matters more than frequency. 1-2 quality articles/month is good.

**Q: Can I guarantee #1 ranking?**
A: No one can guarantee rankings. We can optimize and execute strategy.
