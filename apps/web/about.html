About Unidesk

We're building the future of CRM and messaging for modern businesses.

Our Mission

At Unidesk, we believe every business deserves access to world-class customer relationship management tools. Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs and sales teams with intelligent solutions that drive growth, improve efficiency, and deliver exceptional customer experiences.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Unidesk was born from the frustration of watching small and medium businesses struggle with fragmented tools. We saw teams juggling multiple platforms for CRM, messaging, and customer communication, wasting time on manual processes instead of focusing on growth.

We set out to build an integrated platform that combines the power of a modern CRM with official WhatsApp and Instagram Business API support, giving teams everything they need in one unified dashboard.
